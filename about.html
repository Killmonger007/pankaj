About Me

Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Pankaj Meharchandani, a UI/UX designer with over 5 years of experience in the industry. I have worked with a variety of clients, from startups to established companies, and I have helped them design digital products that are not only visually appealing but also user-friendly and intuitive.

What Do I Do?

As a UI/UX designer, my job is to create digital products that provide a great user experience. This involves a range of tasks, from conducting user research and creating wireframes to designing high-fidelity mockups and collaborating with developers to ensure that the final product is just as we envisioned it.
